MISSION & VISION

At Porsche ,Our mission is to spark a love for driving by creating extraordinary experiences that go beyond the ordinary. Our vision drives us to revolutionize the future of transportation, leading in innovation, design, and performance.

OUR STORY

The Porsche story is one of passion, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of automotive excellence. It began in 1948 with the introduction of the iconic 356 by Ferdinand "Butzi" Porsche. Since then, each chapter has been marked by pushing boundaries, setting new standards, and creating vehicles embodying the driving spirit, including the legendary 911. Venturing into SUVs like the Cayenne and Macan showcased adaptability to meet global needs. Beyond building cars, Porsche crafts experiences that elevate driving, evident in Le Mans victories, development of electric vehicles like the Taycan, and commitment to sustainability.
